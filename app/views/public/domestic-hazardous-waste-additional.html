What is a domestic hazardous waste transfer?

This refers to the legal, documented process of moving dangerous waste produced in households (or by tradespeople at homes) to authorized disposal or treatment facilities.

What is hazardous waste?

Hazardous waste is any discarded material that poses a substantial or potential threat to public health or the environment due to its chemical, biological, or physical properties.

What does recovery mean?

Waste recovery treats waste to serve a useful purpose, replacing raw materials and reducing landfill use through methods like energy generation (incineration), recycling, composting, and material reprocessing.
